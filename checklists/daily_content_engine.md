# Daily Content Engine Checklist

## Objective
Maintain daily visibility while building Angel Precision.

The build itself becomes the marketing engine.

## Minimum Daily Output
- 2 X posts
- 2 LinkedIn posts
- 1 ops note
- 1 proof artifact saved
- 5 lead follow-ups
- 1 waitlist CTA mention

## Weekly Output
- 1 Substack article
- 1-2 YouTube videos
- 1 operational breakdown post
- 1 founder lesson post

## Daily Workflow
### Morning
1. Review premarket readiness.
2. Save one dashboard screenshot.
3. Write one raw founder note.
4. Generate morning LinkedIn + X posts.
5. Publish before market open.

### Midday
1. Log operational observations.
2. Save notable rejection/monitoring events.
3. Capture screenshots if relevant.

### After Market Close
1. Run EOD Proof Report.
2. Generate afternoon LinkedIn + X posts.
3. Generate follow-up drafts.
4. Queue Substack/YouTube ideas.
5. Save proof artifacts.

## Content Themes
- execution discipline
- monitoring
- risk controls
- rejection logic
- live refinement
- founder pressure
- building under uncertainty
- dashboards and visibility
- operational trust

## Never Post
- exact client PnL claims
- guaranteed results
- fake urgency
- unrealistic performance promises
- screenshots exposing client information

## Goal
Visibility compounds.
The market cannot notice infrastructure it never sees.
